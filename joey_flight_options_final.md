## 🏆 RECOMMENDED: Check These First

### 1. Southwest Airlines (southwest.com)
**Why Southwest is Great for Groups:**
- ✅ NO change fees (perfect if plans change)
- ✅ 2 FREE checked bags per person
- ✅ Good on-time performance
- ✅ Direct flights LAS→BNA available

**Expected Price Range**: $280-380 round-trip
- Look for "Wanna Get Away" fares
- Early morning flights (6-8 AM) typically cheapest

### 2. Frontier Airlines (flyfrontier.com)
**Budget Option (but watch the extras!):**
- ✅ Ultra-low base fares
- ✅ Direct flights available
- ⚠️ Base fare is bare-bones (extras cost more)

**Expected Price Range**: $180-320 base + $50-100 extras = $230-420 total
- Consider "WORKS" bundle for groups
- Calculate TOTAL cost including seat selection, carry-on, etc.

1. **Be Flexible with Times**: Early morning (6-8 AM) or late evening flights cost less
